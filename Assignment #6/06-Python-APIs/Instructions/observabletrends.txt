Three observable trends: 
    1. There is a cubic linear regression in the Latitude vs Temperature scatterplot. According to this plot, countries with lower latitudes are hotter, or higher in Fahrenheit.
    2. According to the Humidity vs Latitude scatterplot, there is a higher concentration of Humidity levels closer to the Latitude of 0.  
    3. Windspeed data is more concentrated in higher latitude levels according to the Windspeed vs Latitude scatterplot.
